Wordington Model
The Wordington Model is the result of many years of extensive research. Our quest on a model that would make education meaningful enabled us to think of ways and means to generate knowledge from lived experiences.

As the model speaks, wisdom prompts to acquire necessary knowledge, unlike the traditional educational approach of wisdom controlled by knowledge. This means your professional experience has the potential to generate knowledge, and at the same time decide on what knowledge needs to be acquired.


The new ‘self’ formed from the combination of wisdom and knowledge help individuals to put it into action, which is the application into professional context.
The model provides continuous opportunity to reflect on the applied professional situation so as to generate knowledge from it. The knowledge thus created provides enrichment to the self, and new knowledge for the seekers.

The whole process of learning and knowledge creation is framed in and enriched with an open qualitative approach (Grounded Theory), enriched with case studies and integrated with constant application.
Wordington envisages its candidates to develop themselves as ambassadors of knowledge, through this model.
