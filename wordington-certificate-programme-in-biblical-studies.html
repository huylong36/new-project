Wordington Certificate Programme in Biblical Studies
Certificate Programmes
Wordington Certificate Programme in Biblical studies is designed to develop and establish a strong foundation in selective knowledge, understanding and application of key Biblical concepts.
Wordington offers a range of Certificate programmes for you to advance in Biblical studies. This programme is meant for beginners who are aiming to build their understanding of Biblical knowledge and build a ‘call-oriented’ career in Christian ministry.
