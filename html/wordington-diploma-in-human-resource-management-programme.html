Wordington Diploma in Human Resource Management Programme
Graduate Diploma in Human Resource Management
Wordington Graduate Programme in HRM is designed to capture the professional experiences of candidates from various work situations and help enable them to process those experiences in the development of future HR Managers. Our Experience Enriched Knowledge approach reflects our model of developing ingenious and bespoke learning.
Graduate Diploma in Human Resource Management is aimed to equip candidates with agile skills to lead the workforce in organisations. .
Wordington graduate diploma programme is suitable for people working in any profession and is a great opportunity to build up and boost your HRM skills.
The programme is suitable for those wishing to further develop their understanding of HRM so as to effectively manage people in organisations.
The Wordington graduate diploma in HRM comprises of four units which can be credited towards Wordington Master of Human Resource Management.
The units offered are:

Ethics and HRM.

Managing Diversity.

Managing Effective Decision Making.

Managing Teams.
