Wordington Certificate Programme in International English Writing Skills (3 months)
Wordington Certificate Programme in English Writing Skills is designed to develop excellent English writing skills through structural construction of sentences.
The primary objective of this programme is to help candidates to gain confidence in communicating in English, both in the formal and informal settings. This means, this programme will equip you to write emails, send formal letters and draft business proposals, as well as excellent business communication.
Upon completion of this course, candidates will be able to excel in vocabulary to draft meaningful sentences and produce clear and correct writings.
The programme involves a series of practical sessions and will help the candidates to be professional writers.
