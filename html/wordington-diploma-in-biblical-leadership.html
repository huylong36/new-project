Wordington Diploma in Biblical Leadership
Graduate Diploma in Christian Leadership
Wordington Graduate Programme in Biblical studies is designed to capture the experiences of candidates from various Christian ministry situations and help them to process those experiences to develop them as future church leaders. Our Experience Enriched Knowledge approach reflects our model of developing ingenious and bespoke learning.
Graduate Diploma in Christian Leadership is aimed to equip candidates with agile skills to lead the church and Christian organisations.
Wordington graduate diploma programme in Christian leadership is suitable for people in the Christian ministry and is a great opportunity to build up and boost your leadership skills.
The programme is suitable for those wishing to further develop their understanding of Christian leadership so as to effectively lead the Church, Christian community and Christian organisations.
The Wordington graduate diploma in Christian Leadership comprises of four units which can be credited towards Wordington Master of Leadership.
The units offered are:
Ethics and Christian Leadership.

Leading Diversity in the Church.

Christian Leadership and Decision Making.

Leading Christian Ministry Teams.
