Master of Christian Leadership
The Wordington Master of Christian Leadership programme is designed to help candidates to reflect on their own personal leadership capabilities, systematically record those and recycle using Biblical knowledge.
Wordington Master of Christian Leadership programme enable candidates to process their ministry experiences, test them, analyse them by applying Christian leadership principles, and re-apply the processed knowledge to the pastoral ministry.
As candidates have their own unique ministry experiences, Wordington follows a personalised approach in the Master of Christian Leadership programme. This approach involves candidates working with us in our virtual Christian Leadership laboratories and participating in Wordington Christian Ministry Challenge programmes.
The Master of Leadership programme comprises of four units from the Certificate programme and four units from the diploma programme, and four units from the Master of Leadership programme.
The four units offered under the Master of Leadership programme are:
Leading Change in the Church and Organisations.

Leadership Style Specialisation (any one to be chosen from

Transformational and Charismatic Leadership styles).

Christian Leadership Laboratory.

Christian Ministry Challenges.
