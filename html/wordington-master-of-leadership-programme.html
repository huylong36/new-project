Wordington Master of Leadership Programme
Master of Leadership.
The Wordington Master of Leadership programme is designed to help candidates to reflect on their own personal leadership capabilities, systematically record those and recycle using theoretical knowledge.
Wordington Master of Leadership programme enable candidates to process their experiences, test them, analyse them by applying leadership theories, and re-apply the processed knowledge in professional situations.
As candidates have their own unique experiences, Wordington follow a personalised approach in the Master of Leadership programme. This approach involves candidates working with us in our virtual Leadership laboratories and participating in Wordington Executive Challenge programmes. .
The Master of Leadership programme comprises of four units from the Certificate programme and four units from the diploma programme, and four units from the Master of Leadership programme.
The four units offered under the Master of Leadership programme are:
Leading Change.

Leadership Style Specialisation (any one to be chosen from Transformational, Autocratic or Charismatic Leadership styles).

Leadership Laboratory.

Leadership Executive Challenge.
