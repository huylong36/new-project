Wordington Doctorate Programme in Human Resource Management.
Doctorate in Human Resource Management
Wordington Doctorate is a highly specialised (4-10 years duration) programme designed for established HRM professionals with extensive experience in senior HRM roles.
Please refer to Wordington Model to see how the research is designed.
The Wordington doctoral programme is your unique journey through the world of knowledge and experience interwoven.
As part of this programme you will lead by yourselves and Wordington will support you in this doctoral journey.
