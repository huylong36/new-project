Wordington Doctorate Programme in Biblical studies
Doctorate in Leadership
Wordington Doctorate is a highly specialised (2-5 years duration) programme designed for established Christian leaders.
Please refer to Wordington Model to see how the research is designed.
The Wordington doctoral programme is your unique journey through the world of Biblical knowledge and experiences interwoven.
As part of this programme you will lead by yourselves and Wordington will support you in your doctoral journey.
