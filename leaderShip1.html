Graduate Programmes
Wordington Graduate Leadership Programme is designed to capture the experiences of candidates from various leadership situations and help them to process those experiences to develop them as future leaders. Our Experience Enriched Knowledge approach reflects our model of developing ingenious and bespoke learning.
Wordington Graduate Programme offers:
Graduate Diploma in Leadership
is aimed to equip candidates with agile skills to lead organisations and the society.
Wordington graduate diploma programmes are suitable for people working in any profession and is a great opportunity to build up and boost your leadership skills.
The programme is suitable for those wishing to further develop their understanding of leadership styles so as to effectively lead others and their businesses and organisations.
The Wordington graduate diploma in Leadership comprises of four units which can be credited towards Wordington Master of Leadership.
The units offered are:
Ethics and Leadership.
Leading Diversity.
Leadership and Decision Making.
Leading Teams.
Master of Leadership
The Wordington Master of Leadership programme is designed to help candidates to reflect on their own personal leadership capabilities, systematically record those and recycle using theoretical knowledge.
Wordington Master of Leadership programme enable candidates to process their experiences, test them, analyse them by applying leadership theories, and re-apply the processed knowledge in professional situations.
As candidates have their own unique experiences, Wordington follow a personalised approach in the Master of Leadership programme. This approach involves candidates working with us in our virtual Leadership laboratories and participating in Wordington Executive Challenge programmes.
The Master of Leadership programme comprises of four units from the Certificate programme and four units from the diploma programme, and four units from the Master of Leadership programme.
The four units offered under the Master of Leadership programme are:
Leading Change.
Leadership Style Specialisation (any one to be chosen from
Transformational, Autocratic or Charismatic Leadership styles).
Leadership Laboratory.
Leadership Executive Challenge.
Doctorate in Leadership
Wordington Doctorate is a highly specialised (4-10 years duration) programme designed for established leaders in their chosen profession.
Please refer to Wordington Model to see how the research is designed.
The Wordington doctoral programme is your unique journey through the world of knowledge and experiences interwoven.
As part of this programme you will lead by yourselves and Wordington will support you in your doctoral journey.
