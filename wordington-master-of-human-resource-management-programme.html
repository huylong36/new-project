Master of Human Resource Management
The Wordington Master of HRM is carefully designed to help candidates to reflect on their own personal managerial capabilities, systematically record those and recycled with upgraded knowledge.
Wordington Master of HRM programme enable candidates to process their experiences, test them, analyse them by applying HRM theories, and then apply the processed knowledge into professional situations.

This approach involves candidates working together with us in our virtual HRM laboratories and participating in Wordington Executive Challenge programmes.
The Master of HRM programme comprises of four units from the Certificate programme and four units from the diploma programme, and four units from the Master of HRM programme.
The four units offered under the Master of HRM programme are:
Managing People to Organisational Change.

Evaluation of HRM.

HRM Laboratory.

Managing Executive Challenges.
